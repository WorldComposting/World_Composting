🔥 Hot composting is the fastest way to turn organic waste into finished compost — in as little as 18-21 days if managed well! By maintaining a pile temperature of 130-160°F (55-71°C), thermophilic bacteria break down materials rapidly, killing weed seeds and pathogens in the process. The secret? Balancing carbon and nitrogen at a 25-30:1 ratio, keeping the pile at least 3x3x3 feet to retain heat, and turning at the right moments. Follow this checklist to build, manage, and harvest a properly cooked pile!
