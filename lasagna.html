Lasagna Composting Checklist

Build no-dig garden beds from the ground up.

Sheet mulching — also called lasagna composting — builds rich garden beds right where you want them. No tilling, no digging. Just layer organic materials like cardboard, compost, straw, and leaves directly on top of grass or soil. Worms and microbes do the work of turning it all into dark, crumbly earth. Build it in fall or early spring, let the layers break down, and plant directly into pure compost when it's ready.
