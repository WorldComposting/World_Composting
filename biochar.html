🔥 Biochar is a highly porous form of carbon created through pyrolysis—burning organic material in a low-oxygen environment. Think of it as a "permanent sponge" for your soil: once added, it stays there for hundreds of years, providing a massive surface area for beneficial microbes to live in and helping your soil retain moisture and nutrients.
